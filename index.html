Graduate Outcomes Dashboard — MSU Career Center

This project analyzes Graduation Year 2024 student outcomes at MSU to provide actionable insights for colleges, departments, and students. Using institutional graduation data, I developed an interactive Power BI dashboard that highlights key alumni metrics such as employment rate, continuing education rate, knowledge rate, and overall post-graduation success. The dashboard enables stakeholders to explore alumni employment status, top hiring employers, job roles, geographic distribution, and salary expectations. It also provides insights into graduates pursuing further education, including top destination institutions and their locations. This project supports data-driven decision-making for academic units while offering prospective and current students transparency into career and education outcomes after graduation. The dashboard is published on the MSU Institutional Research website.
